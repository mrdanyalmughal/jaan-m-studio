Our Heritage

Three generations of colour and craft.

Since 1984, Jaan M. Studios has grown from a small Lahore painting house into one of Pakistan's most trusted names in painting and interior design. Our reputation was not bought. It was earned, one perfectly finished wall at a time.

That trust is why families, hoteliers, developers, and institutions return to us, and why our work stands on some of the country's most recognised addresses.
